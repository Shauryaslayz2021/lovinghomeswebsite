ABOUT US

Based in Hong Kong, Loving Homes is a hotel for dogs which provides luxurious, pet-friendly and premium quality services. The services we provide show great care, dedication and love for the pets and we tend to treat them as our own whenever we get a new customer. The facilities we offer are groomers and parlors, rooms with climate controls and underfloor heating, an indoor play barn, woodland walks and a responsive staff and veterinary that is available for all 24 hours.

We know that since every dog is special in their own ways, we ensure that through the owners the pets have all the necessities they require. The owners can chooose from a range of packages that we offer that can be tailored for their pets. The packages are: Day, Classic, Premium or a Custom package that they can make for their pets. Our goal is to make their stay memorable and they are always welcome again.
